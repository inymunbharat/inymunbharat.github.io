About Us

International Youth Model United Nations, Bharat. Is the brainchild of a highly passionate and phenomenal youth leader. Making sure that every effort has been towards providing an accessible and high-quality platform for the students to exhibit their inner diplomats, debaters and leaders.

The world has trampled upon and forgotten the art of diplomacy and it’s brilliance in resolving even some of the most difficult situations which befall a country or a region. The world is slowly slipping from the pedestal of peace and harmony deteriorating towards wars leading to destruction and inhumanness violence. It is thus of the need of the hour to build up this temperament of peace and diplomacy in the minds of our future leaders, the students of today. This process is the only way forward to make sure our beautiful world together with all of its people reaches its zenith. Discussion and deliberation are of utmost importance between countries and regions for a peaceful and beautiful world.

The INYMUN, Bharat conference in its previous editions has had committees of exceptional quality giving the delegates a highly authentic experience of the proceedings and process of UN with highly qualified and excellent Executive Board members. All this complemented by a secretariat with the work ethic and quality par excellence made up of some of the most experienced people from the MUN circuit. This all brought out an excellent platform for giving the participating delegates an incredible amount of knowledge about the ongoing political environment of the country and the world, with some highly productive discussion and deliberation on related issues.

UN Women

“No country can ever truly flourish if it stifles the potential of its women and deprives itself of the contributions of half its citizens.”

With a firm belief in these lines quoted by Michelle Obama, we at INYMUN have dedicated our efforts to provide a platform for individual students to discuss the crucial topic of menstruating and pregnant women in poverty-stricken areas with special emphasis on the pandemic and conditions of hygiene in government hospitals.

The issues of women have always been neglected and highly stigmatized, menstrual health and hygiene is one which has become a victim of this neglect. The pandemic led to immense problems in getting medical aid due to risk of infection.These issues had more devastating effects in the poverty-stricken areas. It’s of utmost importance that a solution is found as continued neglect could do irreversible damage on the health of millions of women across the world.

SPECPOL

“Freedom is the open window through which pours the sunlight of the human spirit and human dignity”

The Special Political and Decolonization Committee or SPECPOL aims through it’s session to bring this freedom to the nations which have fallen under the trap of neo-colonialism by discussing the rise of this phenomenon and ways to combat it helping the participants understand this rejuvenated form of the heinous historical atrocity of colonialism which brought unspeakable amount of suffering to so many.

It is of the utmost importance that we understand how devastating the effects of neo-colonialism could be, as failure to do so could send many countries into the fathomless pit of devastation and suffering. The SPECOL shall analyse the parties involved and their roles in reducing the risk of neo-colonialism and help the countries who have fallen victim recover. Such discussion shall aid the minds of our youth to value the freedom they have attained.

AIPPM

In the words of Mr.Dwight.D.Eisenhower, “Politics is ought to be a part-time profession of every citizen who would protect the rights and privileges of free people and who would protect what is good and fruitful in our national heritage”

To promote this thought, INYMUN aims to give voice to the youth on pressing issues like the tense situation at Line of Actual Control(LAC) and the introduction of New Education Policy 2020(NEP). India-China Relations have always been a very sensitive topic. Frequent border standoffs have made the situation more complex. The standoff in Galwan Area acted as a spark in the haystack. Both the neighbours are engaged in an indirect conflict on several non-conventional grounds. Still, the further approach towards China is not discussed properly.

The last education policy was implemented way back in 1986. Since then the Indian education system is more or less the same. This caused a huge fiasco as the existing education system was not keeping up its pace with modern requirements. For that, a New Education Policy got approved by the Union Cabinet on 29th July 2020. The policy, promises for betterment and to open new horizons in the education system. However, this NEP is facing criticism from the opposition party. So far the facts on this issue are not motivating for the Central Government.

To discuss these extremely power-packed and tense topics, we are proud to release All India Political Party Meet(AIPPM) as our second Committee.

It’s your call now, if not us then who? If not now then when?
