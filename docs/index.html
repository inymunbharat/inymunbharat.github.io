About Us

International Youth SUMMIT, Bharat is a deeply passionate and phenomenal youth leader's brainchild. All over the years we have been ensuring that every attempt has been made to provide the students with an open and high-quality forum to showcase their internal diplomats, debaters and leaders. In its previous editions, the INY SUMMIT, Bharat conference had committees of excellent consistency, offering participants a highly authentic understanding of UN procedures and processes with highly trained and exemplary members of the Executive Board. All this was supplemented by a secretariat made up by some of the most seasoned individuals from the MUN circuit with professional standards and efficiency par excellence.

The Youth Parliament

Deliberation on changing dynamics of personal laws in India with special reference to the Uniform Civil Code.

There is concern among religious minority groups regarding the adoption of the Universal Civil Code, as they claim it would infringe on their freedom to exercise religion and a Uniform Civil Code may become skewed against the laws of a large society. Apart from that, people are deeply bound to their deeply religious traditions and they are fearful of losing their religious identities if their customary rules are repealed, and this fear affects not just minority religious communities, but also religious groups of the majority of the population.

The issues of women have always been neglected and highly stigmatized, menstrual health and hygiene is one which has become a victim of this neglect. The pandemic led to immense problems in getting medical aid due to risk of infection.These issues had more devastating effects in the poverty-stricken areas. It’s of utmost importance that a solution is found as continued neglect could do irreversible damage on the health of millions of women across the world.

UNHRC

Since the adoption of the United Nations Charter in 1945, the international community, in recognition of the vital importance of securing respect for human rights and freedom from fear, has developed an impressive body of international human rights law. Among the most fundamental of all human rights is the right to personal liberty. One significant dimension of this right is freedom from arbitrary arrest and detention.

United State Senate

Evaluating Police and Social Reforms in the United States with Special Reference to the Black Lives Matter Movement.
Determining the Status and rights of Transgender citizen in the United States of America.

The assassination of African-American George Floyd by a rogue white police officer has sparked mass unrest in the United States, prompting calls for police reform. Democrats in the United States Congress have introduced a bill that will make law enforcement officers responsible for wrongdoing. Other ideas include defunding police departments and redirecting some of the funds to social welfare and healthcare, particularly in light of Covid-19. The city of Minneapolis, where Floyd was killed, is considering disbanding the police department and replacing it with a new public-safety model. There's no doubt that American police departments are militarised, contributing to a harsh policing strategy.

The Biden campaign discussed transgender rights early on. In his victory address, he became the first president to express his commitment to the nation. Following his victory in November, then-President-elect Biden tweeted that his administration "will see you, listen to you, and fight for not only your safety, but also the dignity and justice you have been denied."
